Who are we?

We are a company that sells quality pop corn. It stated with a young man who was obsessed with popcorn. He met someone who believed in his passion and together they co-founded this company.

What do we do exactly?

We produce, package and distribute of popcorn. We develop custom popcorn recipes. We also sell popcorn making utensils and prop accessories such as key chains.

We plant our own popcorn

All of our popped corn are made from popcorn that we grow at our local farm. All seasoning and flavorings are also made from scratch using organic products.
